| Platform | Registered Users | Monthly Active Sellers | Trend |
|----------|-----------------|----------------------|-------|
| **Poshmark** | 80M+ | ~5 million | Declining (3 consecutive years of MAU decline) |
| **eBay (US)** | ~95M | Tens of millions | Stable |
| **Mercari (US)** | 20M+ | 4.3M MAU | Mixed (revenue up 11%, MAU down 4%) |
| **Depop** | 35M | 2.5M active sellers | Growing strongly (+31.6% GMS YoY) |

### Platform Trajectories

**Poshmark (Declining)**
- Acquired by Naver for $1.2B (Jan 2023)
- CEO Manish Chandra stepped down Aug 2025
- Monthly active sellers declining for 3 consecutive years
- Controversial policy changes: removed bulk sharing (Nov 2025, restored Dec 2025), switched to algorithmic "For You" feed (Oct 2025), new 60-day relist policy (May 2025)
- GMV growth slowed to 3.1% YoY

**Mercari (Mixed)**
- Record revenue in Q2 FY2026 (up 15% YoY consolidated)
- US revenue up 11%, GMV up 12% to $196M
- But US MAU edged down 4% to 4.3M
- Achieved first-ever US full-year profit

**Depop (Growing)**
- 2024 GMS: $789M, up 31.6% YoY
- Q2 2025 GMS grew 35.3% YoY
- Clear winner among platforms in growth trajectory
- Acquired by Etsy for $1.6B (2021)

## The Reseller Tool Market

### Key Stat: 78% of serious resellers list on 3+ platforms. Cross-listed items see 2.4x more sales.

**Estimated serious multi-platform resellers in US:** 1-3 million

**Combined paying users of ALL reseller tools:** Under 100,000

**Market penetration: 3-10%** — the tool market is extremely early.

### No published TAM exists for reseller tools specifically.

**Bottom-up estimate:**
- If 1-2M resellers would pay $25/month: $300M-$600M/year SAM
- Current served market: ~$30-100M/year (all tools combined)
- Massive whitespace

## Growth Drivers

1. **Tariffs** — 59% of consumers say they'll turn to secondhand as prices rise. ThredUp CEO: "a structural tailwind"
2. **Gen Z preference** — 48% of young consumers look secondhand FIRST
3. **Platform diversification** — As Poshmark weakens, resellers spread to more platforms, making cross-listing tools more necessary
4. **Sustainability mainstream** — No longer niche; default behavior for younger demographics
5. **Economic pressure** — Inflation driving budget-conscious behavior across demographics

### How Existing Companies Market

**Vendoo:** Affiliate program (15-20% commission), content marketing blog, YouTube (~5.26K subs), ~250 new subscribers/month

**List Perfectly:** 20% lifetime recurring affiliate commission, free Facebook group community, "The Seller Community Podcast," ambassador program with beta access

**Both rely primarily on reseller influencer affiliates** as their main growth channel. Generous recurring commissions incentivize YouTubers and TikTokers to create review content.

## Key Analyst Perspectives

**Bull case (majority view):**
- Resale grows 2-3x faster than first-hand market through 2027 (McKinsey/BoF)
- Tariffs act as structural tailwind for the entire sector
- App downloads for resale platforms surged in Q1 2025 during tariff announcements

**Bear case / nuances:**
- Earlier projections revised downward ($82B by 2026 → actual ~$60-65B)
- Individual platforms can plateau (Poshmark) even while overall market grows
- Growth rates moderating from ~11% to ~6% by 2029
